Honey

For millennia, honey was only considered a sweetener, whereas today we know that it contains many other substances that are beneficial for people. It is an excellent nutrient and calmative. Owing to its antibacterial and antioxidant properties, it is widely used in folk medicine. Being located in the center of Moldova we proudly produce 100% pure acacia, linden and sunflower honey.

Acacia Honey

Known for its beautiful light golden color and incredible clarity it is the lightest tasting honey in the world. From the pristine acacia forests in Moldova our acacia honey is raw to retain all of its natural nutritional value and health benefits. The natural sugar profile of acacia honey resists crystallization. In spite of its sweetness, Acacia honey has a very low sucrose content and a high fructose level, so it is the best choice for diabetics.  It is known for its therapeutic qualities because it cleanses the liver, conditions the intestines, and is beneficial for the respiratory system due to antibacterial properties –  another benefit of honey.

Linden Honey

The intensity of the smell and taste is much stronger than it would be judged by the color of linden honey. It has a fresh woody with a hint of mint, balsamic and camphor aroma. Linden honey is sweet, sometimes with a touch of bitterness. After it you can always feel persistent aftertaste and light astringency. About 3 months after harvesting the linden honey it begins to crystallize: it thickens and loses its transparency. But white honey does not lose its useful properties and taste. Its anti-bacterial properties are the highest compared with other types of products of bees and is used in remedies for cold and gastrointestinal tract diseases. The composition of honey has almost a full range of vitamins and minerals, essential oils and trace elements, so the white honey has amazing properties in cosmetology. Not surprisingly, this natural product is commonly used in the manufacture of cosmetics.

Sunflower Honey

The rich Moldovan soil allows farmers to grow acres of sunflowers and that is where we take our bees during the month of July. Sunflower honey has a floral aroma with a fresh, herbaceous flavor. This type of honey does crystalize, the process might take from twenty days to two months and forms large crystals. Fеw реорlе knоw thаt ѕunflоwеr hоnеу іѕ аbѕоlutеlу рurе. Наrmful ѕubѕtаnсеѕ аnd роllutаntѕ frоm thе ѕоіl аrе rеtаіnеd іn thе ѕunflоwеr ѕtеm аnd ѕо thе nесtаr thаt bееѕ соllесt rеmаіnѕ іntасt. Іt іѕ аlѕо duе tо thе fасt thаt ѕunflоwеr сrорѕ аrе nоt trеаtеd wіth іnѕесtісіdеѕ. Ѕunflоwеr hоnеу hаѕ а hеаlіng еffесt wіth rеѕресt tо gаѕtrоіntеѕtіnаl dіѕеаѕеѕ, thе rеѕріrаtоrу ѕуѕtеm, thе kіdnеуѕ, ѕkіn рrоblеmѕ, but іt іѕ uѕеful еѕресіаllу fоr thе hеаrt аnd thе сіrсulаtоrу ѕуѕtеm, аnd іѕ а vаluаblе tооl іn thе рrеvеntіоn аnd fіght аgаіnѕt саrdіоvаѕсulаr dіѕеаѕеѕ.
