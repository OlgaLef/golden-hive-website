Beeswax and Candles

Our beeswax is 100% pure that has been filtered twice preserving its amber color and a hint of honey fragrance. The beeswax is ready to use for candle making, crafts, sewing, quilting, woodworking, archery and more. Because burning beeswax releases negative ions that neutralize bacteria, dust, odors, dander, and other undesirable particles in the air we have available a beautiful collection of 100% pure beeswax candles. Our candle may even help ease the symptoms of asthma, allergies, or hay fever, by offsetting the dust and allergens in the air.
